Verify your National ID

Your NID is the anchor of your tax identity. A unique TIN will be auto-generated and permanently linked to your NID.

Each NID can only be linked to one TIN. If you already have a TIN, log in using your existing credentials instead.
NID number is required. NID must be exactly 10 or 17 digits.
Date of birth is required.
Gender is required.

Company Registration Details

Your RJSC registration number is the anchor of your corporate tax identity. A unique TIN will be auto-generated and linked to your company.

Each RJSC number can only be linked to one TIN. If your company already has a TIN, use the existing login credentials.
Company name is required.
RJSC registration number is required. Enter a valid RJSC number (6-20 characters, alphanumeric).
Incorporation date is required.
Authorized person name is required.
Authorized person NID is required. NID must be exactly 10 or 17 digits.
